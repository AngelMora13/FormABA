What is this?

Web application, designed to an easy evaluation of feed´s quality features, before its elaboration. Until now, quality features are: moisture percent, Crude protein percent, Crude fat percent like Ether extract, crude fiber percent and ash percent.

Formulate

Can be Formulate Feed (F.F.) or Formulate Ingredients (F.I.).

If you arent login, the list of feedstocks are a local feedstocks which was taken from Fundación Española para el Desarrollo de la Nutrición Animal, FEDNA.

Note: all feedstocks are not taken.

if you are logged in, local list of feedstocks are not display instead you can add, modify and delete you own feedstocks.

When you formulate, you need select at least one feedstock to F.F. and two for F.I. and select the method suitable.

You can see more features of feedstocks doing click on it name.

Formulate Feed

Predict the nutritional profile of feed through selection some feedstocks and it amount of mass at mix.

On screen you will see the feedstock selected and a box where you can put it amount of mass.

To predict, click on Predict button.

Formulate Ingredients

Predicts the mass amount of every feedstock through the nutritional profile of mix.

After selecting feedstocks, you will see a table where you put the nutritional profile expected and the maximum allowed.

Rules:

  • Every nutritional feature expected cannot be zero (0) or negative
  • Every nutritional feature expected cannot be higer than the maximum allowed
  • You need at least two feedstocks to formulate ingredients

Considerations: Feedstocks selected and nutritional profile can will not match with a result due to nature of feedstocks, example:

Soy has about 45% of crude protein and it is higher tha other feedstocks you selected, then you need 55% of crude protein as nutritional feature expected. This make impossible reach an optimum result because the max value is 45%.

Recommendations when there is not result:

  • Increase the maximum value allowed.
  • Reduce the expected value.
  • Select other feedstocks.

After having a result, you will see the amount of mass of feedstocks, the calculate value and the expected value.

For any drawbacks, plase contact.

Login / Register

To do login just click on login button and enter credentials.

to Register, click on register in login page and fill the form.

After login, you will se the user button, add / modify button and delete button.

When you are logged in, you cannot access to local feedstocks, just can access to you own database.

User

This page show the data form when you register the account and the amount of feedstocks you have.

Add / Modify

You can add and modify you own feedstocks.

Rules:

  • The name cannot be repeated or empty
  • The features cannot be negatives

Delete

You can delete you own feedstocks.
